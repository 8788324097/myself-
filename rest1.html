About Us

Located in the heart of India, PJ’s Restaurant brings a modern twist to traditional Indian cuisine. Founded by food lovers and run by India’s top chefs, we serve dishes that speak to the soul.

Our Chefs

Chef Sanjeev Kapoor

Sanjeev Kapoor is the most prominent and iconic personality in the Indian culinary landscape. He first shot to fame for hosting a highly popular food show named Khana Khazana which ran for 17 years and brought the nuances of top-notch cooking to households in India and abroad. Kapoor has developed his own curated recipes across cuisines, right from Indian to Continental.

Chef Vikas Khanna

Vikas Khanna is a Michelin Star celebrity chef and also a restaurateur and film maker. He graduated in hotel management from Manipal Academy of Higher Education, before moving to USA to study at the Culinary Institute of America. Khanna worked in a number of high-end hotels in India and USA prior to opening his own restaurant in Dubai named Kinara. He followed it up with two more restaurants, Ellora in Dubai and Bungalow in New York City.

Chef Ranveer Brar

Ranveer Brar is a celebrity chef, television personality, restaurateur and author. He began his career as a chef with the Taj Hotel in Delhi, and became the youngest executive chef in India when he was appointed to this position at the Raddison Blue Hotel in Noida. Brar opened a Franco-Asian restaurant in Boston which won several awards. Presently, he is the Senior Executive Chef at Novotel, Juhu Beach.
